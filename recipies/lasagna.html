Lasagna

Source: https://www.tasteofhome.com/

A classic meat lasagna is made by layering a rich Bolognese-style meat sauce, a creamy ricotta cheese filling, and lasagna noodles before baking until golden and bubbly. This recipe details the process for making a hearty, crowd-pleasing lasagna.

Ingredianta

  • Meat sauce
  • Egg
  • cheese
  • Salt
  • Noodles

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the ground beef and Italian sausage, breaking up the meat with a wooden spoon as it cooks. Drain any excess fat.
  3. Add the diced onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for 1 more minute until fragrant.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper
